## 💡 【附录：Q&A 答辩核心防御策略】

### Q1：如何测量记忆 (Measuring Memory)?
*   **核心词**: **Information Retention** (信息保持度) vs Long-term memory.
*   **要点**: Measure short/mid-term recall (e.g., immediate test post-visit) ➔ Avoid confounding variables (时间流逝噪音).

### Q2：Phase 3 实验变量从何而来 (Where do variables come from)?
*   **核心词**: Phase 2 co-creation workshop (共创决定).
*   **预备变量 (Example factors)**:
    *   **Factor A (Spatial)**: Eye-switch distance (植物-牌示视线切换距离：近/远).
    *   **Factor B (Information)**: Text density (文本视觉密度：高/低).

### Q3：真实温室实验的噪音控制 (Greenhouse noise control)?
*   **核心词**: **Within-subjects design** (被试内设计) & **Latin square** (拉丁方平衡).
*   **平衡**: Sacrifice minor lab precision ➔ gain high **Ecological validity** (生态效度).
